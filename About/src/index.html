Lobitos and Piedritas. It'll blow your mind.

Lobitos and Piedritas are neighbouring communities on the northern coast of Peru. Tourism is a growing economic opportunity for this arid region, due to the beneficial conditions for sports such as surfing. However, doing this sustainably to protect the local natural habitat, and including the local community in the economic benefit, is not without challenge.
